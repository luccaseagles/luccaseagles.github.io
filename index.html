Intel Group Project: Collaborative Robotics

Research on FPGA implementation of Collaborative robots.

A group project with Intel in which we researched, simulated, and developed collaborative robot safety functions according to industry standard certification documents. We developed one specific method to detect the position of the robot and stop it before a collision occurs.

Team members: Elliott Yu, Hisham Nasser, Terry Peng, Ebhrahem Sherwi

JAPFA Comfeed: Smart Scale

Industry sponsored prototype.

I worked with a team of 6 Imperial and UCL students to develop a “smart scale prototype” for JAPFA COMFEED Myanmar. The scale sits at the bottom of the coop, processes the weight data to find an estimate of the average coop weight, and wirelessly submits the results to the farm owner.

Team members: Will Davies, Noe Guisset, Giovanni Bond, Manith Adikari, Arnav Bisoi

Fuzzy Logic Dual Axis Solar Panel

Project develeped in renewable energy research lab for the University of Sao Paulo

Dual axis solar panel sun tracker project made during my summer internship at UNESP University in Sao Paulo Brazil. Implements fuzzy logic to ensure precise sun tracking even when individual LDRs have different light response characteristics.

Smart Hive

Connected bee hive monitoring system

Beekeepers only have access to a limited amount of information on their hive’s health, as inspecting a hive too frequently disturbs the bees. Our project was to design an electronic hive monitoring system which can assist bee keepers in identifying and treating problems within the hive.

Team members: Joao Baptista, Noet Guisset, Edoardo David Santi, Albert Fleta Moya, Anselm Lohmann

Autonomous Robot

High School Personal Project

This was a personal project that I worked on at the end of High School. My goal was to build a robot which could autonomously navigate between two pre-defined points in an unknown environment. The robot reads data from its wheel encoders, and uses distance sensors to gain information about its environment. It uses this information to follow a state machine and ultimately move to its target location.
